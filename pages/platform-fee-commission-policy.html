This Platform Fee & Commission Policy explains how AllLocal may charge platform fees, commissions, payment processing fees, and other applicable charges for using the marketplace.

1. Purpose

AllLocal provides a digital marketplace connecting customers, sellers, and service providers. To maintain, improve, and operate the platform, certain fees or commissions may apply.

2. Types of Fees

  • Platform commission on completed transactions.
  • Payment gateway processing charges where applicable.
  • Service fees for premium platform features.
  • Advertising or promotional listing fees (optional).
  • Subscription plans or membership fees, if introduced.

3. Fee Disclosure

Applicable fees and commissions will be clearly disclosed before they are charged. Sellers and service providers can review applicable charges through the platform where available.

4. Seller & Service Provider Payouts

  • Payouts are generally processed after successful completion of eligible transactions.
  • Applicable platform commissions, taxes, refunds, chargebacks, or statutory deductions may be deducted before payout.
  • Payout schedules may vary depending on verification requirements and payment partners.

5. Taxes

Users remain responsible for complying with applicable tax laws. Where required, applicable taxes may be collected, deducted, or reported in accordance with Indian law.

6. Fee Revisions

AllLocal may revise platform fees or introduce new fee structures in the future. Reasonable notice will be provided where practicable before significant changes become effective.

7. No Hidden Charges

AllLocal aims to maintain transparent pricing. Any applicable fees or commissions will be communicated through the platform before they become payable.
