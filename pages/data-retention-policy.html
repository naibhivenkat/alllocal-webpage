Data Retention Policy

AllLocal • Powered by Swasthik Enterprises

Website: alllocal.in

This Data Retention Policy explains how AllLocal retains, protects, archives, and deletes user information collected through its website and mobile application. This policy should be read together with the Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions.

1. Purpose

AllLocal retains information only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent fraud, maintain security, and enforce platform policies.

2. Information We May Retain

  • User account information.
  • Business, seller, and service provider details.
  • Orders and booking history.
  • Payment and transaction records.
  • Customer support communications.
  • Login, device, and security logs.
  • Invoices and tax-related records where applicable.

3. Retention Period

Retention periods vary depending on the type of information, legal requirements, business needs, fraud prevention, taxation, accounting, dispute resolution, and regulatory compliance. Data may be retained longer where required by law or legitimate business interests.

4. Account Deletion

  • Users may request deletion of their account where permitted.
  • Certain information may continue to be retained if required by applicable law, fraud prevention, accounting, taxation, dispute resolution, or security obligations.
  • Deleted accounts may not be recoverable.

5. Backups

Information may remain in secure backup systems for a limited period after deletion requests to support disaster recovery, security, and business continuity.

6. Security

AllLocal implements reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect retained information against unauthorized access, loss, alteration, or disclosure.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Data Retention Policy from time to time. Continued use of the platform after updates become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
