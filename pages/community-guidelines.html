Community Guidelines

AllLocal • Powered by Swasthik Enterprises

Website: alllocal.in

These Community Guidelines help ensure that AllLocal remains a safe, trustworthy, and respectful marketplace for customers, sellers, service providers, and businesses.

1. Respect Everyone

  • Treat all users with courtesy and professionalism.
  • Do not engage in harassment, bullying, discrimination, hate speech, or abusive behaviour.
  • Respect cultural, religious, and regional diversity.

2. Honest Information

  • Provide accurate account details.
  • Use genuine business information and contact details.
  • Do not impersonate another individual or organization.

3. Listings & Services

  • List only lawful products and services.
  • Use genuine descriptions and images.
  • Do not advertise prohibited, counterfeit, unsafe, or misleading products or services.

4. Reviews & Ratings

  • Leave honest reviews based on actual experiences.
  • Do not post fake reviews or manipulate ratings.
  • Avoid offensive or defamatory content in reviews.

5. Prohibited Activities

  • Fraudulent transactions.
  • Illegal products or services.
  • Spam or unsolicited promotions.
  • Malware, phishing, or attempts to compromise platform security.
  • Uploading false, deceptive, or copyrighted content without permission.
  • Circumventing platform policies or payment systems.

6. Safety

  • Follow applicable laws and regulations.
  • Exercise reasonable caution during deliveries and service appointments.
  • Report suspicious or unsafe behaviour through official support channels.

7. Reporting Violations

Users are encouraged to report suspected fraud, abuse, policy violations, or illegal activity. AllLocal may investigate reports and take appropriate action.

8. Enforcement

Violations of these Community Guidelines may result in warnings, removal of content, temporary suspension, permanent account termination, or reporting to law enforcement where required by law.

9. Changes to These Guidelines

AllLocal may update these Community Guidelines from time to time. Continued use of the platform after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised Guidelines.
