Shipping & Delivery Policy

AllLocal is a technology platform that connects customers with independent sellers and service providers. Delivery of products and fulfillment of services are primarily the responsibility of the respective seller or service provider unless otherwise specified.

1. Delivery Coverage

Delivery availability depends on the seller, service provider, customer location and service area. Not all products or services are available in every location.

2. Delivery Responsibility

  • Sellers are responsible for packing and dispatching products.
  • Service providers are responsible for attending confirmed bookings.
  • Delivery may be performed by the seller, a delivery partner or another authorized representative.

3. Delivery Timelines

Estimated delivery or service times are displayed where available. Actual timelines may vary due to weather conditions, traffic, holidays, availability, operational constraints or other unforeseen circumstances.

4. Delivery Charges

Delivery charges, if applicable, will be displayed before checkout. Charges may vary depending on distance, order value, seller policies and promotional offers.

5. Customer Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate delivery address and contact information.
  • Ensure someone is available to receive the order, where applicable.
  • Inspect the order upon delivery and report genuine issues promptly.

6. Failed Delivery

If delivery cannot be completed because of an incorrect address, customer unavailability or inability to contact the customer, the seller may cancel the order or attempt redelivery according to their policy. Additional charges may apply where permitted.

7. Order Tracking

Where supported, customers may receive updates regarding the status of their order or service booking through the AllLocal platform.

8. Delays

While every effort is made to complete deliveries on time, AllLocal cannot guarantee delivery times. Delays caused by events beyond reasonable control do not automatically qualify for compensation.

9. Marketplace Disclaimer

AllLocal provides the digital platform facilitating transactions between buyers, sellers and service providers. Responsibility for product dispatch, delivery and service fulfillment remains with the respective seller or service provider unless otherwise required by applicable law.
