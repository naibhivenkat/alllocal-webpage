This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") explains the standards that all users of the AllLocal website and mobile application must follow. By using AllLocal, you agree to comply with this Policy, the Terms & Conditions, Privacy Policy, and all applicable laws.

1. Purpose

AllLocal is a marketplace that connects customers, sellers, service providers, and local businesses. This policy helps maintain a safe, secure, trustworthy, and lawful environment for everyone using the platform.

2. Acceptable Use

  • Use the platform only for lawful purposes.
  • Provide accurate and truthful information.
  • Respect the rights, privacy, and safety of other users.
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations.

3. Prohibited Activities

  • Selling illegal, counterfeit, stolen, or prohibited products.
  • Offering fraudulent, unsafe, or misleading services.
  • Creating fake accounts or impersonating another person or business.
  • Uploading false, deceptive, or misleading information.
  • Manipulating reviews, ratings, or platform rankings.
  • Sending spam, unsolicited advertisements, or malicious content.
  • Attempting unauthorized access to user accounts or platform systems.
  • Distributing malware, viruses, ransomware, or harmful software.
  • Violating intellectual property rights or privacy rights.
  • Using the platform for money laundering, fraud, or other criminal activities.
  • Harassing, threatening, abusing, or discriminating against any user.
  • Circumventing AllLocal's payment systems or platform policies.

4. Security

  • Do not attempt to hack, scan, disrupt, or overload the platform.
  • Do not interfere with platform availability or performance.
  • Protect your login credentials and OTPs.
  • Immediately report suspected security issues.

5. User Content

Users are responsible for all content they upload, including product listings, service descriptions, photographs, reviews, ratings, and messages. Content must not be unlawful, defamatory, obscene, or infringe third-party rights.

6. Enforcement

AllLocal may investigate suspected violations and may remove content, suspend services, temporarily restrict accounts, permanently terminate accounts, cancel orders, or report unlawful activities to relevant authorities where required by law.

7. Changes to this Policy

AllLocal may update this Acceptable Use Policy from time to time. Continued use of the platform after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
