AllLocal is an app-based e-commerce and local services platform operated by Swasthik Enterprises. We connect customers with local shops, sellers, service providers and businesses across villages, rural areas, towns and cities. AllLocal acts only as a technology platform unless specifically stated otherwise.

We may share limited information with sellers, service providers, logistics partners, payment gateways and government authorities where required by law. We do not sell personal information to third parties.
