1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They help websites remember information about your visit and improve functionality and user experience.

2. Why We Use Cookies

  • To keep you signed in to your account.
  • To remember your preferences and settings.
  • To improve website performance and security.
  • To understand how visitors use our website.
  • To support customer service and platform functionality.

3. Types of Cookies We May Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for login, navigation and core website functions.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember language, preferences and user settings.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand website usage and improve performance.
  • Security Cookies: Help detect fraud and protect user accounts.

5. Managing Cookies
