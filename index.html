FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform.

What is Local Shop Finder?

Local Shop Finder is a platform that connects customers with nearby local businesses, enabling secure online ordering and payments.

Who can use this platform?

Customers, shop owners, retailers, grocery stores, pharmacies, vegetable vendors, and other local businesses.

How are payments processed?

Payments are processed securely through trusted payment gateway partners supporting UPI, Cards, Net Banking, and Wallets.

Can shop owners register?

Yes. Shop owners can register their business and manage products, inventory, and customer orders from their dashboard.
